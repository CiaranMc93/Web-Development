Bio

Ciaran McManus is a software developer who is passionate about everything computer related.

Ciaran is very versatile in many aspects of his abilities. He is able to learn quickly and be useful in many different situations. Time management, problem solving and being a team player are some things that Ciaran prides himself on.

Ciaran has over a year of experience in software development on an industrial code base that is being supplied to one of the leading air carriers in the world.
